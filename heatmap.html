Instructions

The color of heatmap indicates the gaming selling amount of each platform in that year. The x-axis is the year(in step of every 5 years), and the y-axis is different mainstream home video game consoles. The pie charts, when clicking on each platform/year block in th heatmap, are distribution of publisher for games on that platform in that year.

Discussion

From the heatmap, the trend of home gaming consoles is obvious and showing how the market of gaming console shifts.

From the pie charts, we can see that when a console is relatively new to the market, only a few publishers are producing games for that platform, and in 5 years, the publishers could increase largely.

Comprehensively, from the coloring in the heatmap and numbers of categories in pie chart, we can see the most way of playing games at home consoles: for example, at year of 2000, Sony's PlayStaion was dominating the market with large amount of sales and various of publishers of game on it. Soon as 2005, PlayStation2 took over and PlayStation is not in the map anymore; also in the year of 2005 the x360 appears but only had 8 publishers for games on it. In the year of 2010, x360 was selling better and is really close to PlayStation3's sales, but wii was far more popular than PlayStation and x360... It is interesting to see how the gaming consoles come and go and how their companies present new products in the competition.

Planned interactivity

Connection to the project theme

This visualization presents from the perspective of the component of market of gaming console(specifically on the home gaming consoles), and how games on each platform was occupied by various publishers. It is an observation on players, customers, producers and even hardware industries from the gaming console market shifting.
